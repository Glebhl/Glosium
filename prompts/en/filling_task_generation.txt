Create one fill-in-the-blank exercise.

Requirements:
- Write a cohesive paragraph consisting of 1-2 sentences or 3 very short.
- Each blank should involve only one TARGET.
- Spread all of the listed targets evenly across the whole exercise.
- Do not create sentence without any of the listed targets.
- Provide one word bank containing 2-4 extra distractor words.

Output format:
PARAGRAPH: <text with blanks enclosed in square brackets.>
DISTRACTORS:
- ...
- ...

Example output:
PARAGRAPH: The [sky] is blue. The [grass] is green.
DISTRACTORS:
- ski
- gross
